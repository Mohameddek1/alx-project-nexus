# Demo Video Guidelines

Keep the demo video under 5 minutes. Focus only on your project. Suggested structure:

1. Quick intro (10s): project name and what it does
2. Walkthrough (3-4min): show core features, key endpoints, and ERD
3. Deployment check (30s): open hosted link and show live interactions
4. Closing (10s): lessons learned and next steps

Upload the video to YouTube (unlisted) or Google Drive and paste the link below.

Notes:
- Use screen-recording tools like OBS or Loom.
- Add short captions or voice-over describing what's happening.
- Ensure mentors have view access to the link.
